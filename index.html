Over 400 Years of Horology

Somlo Antiques is London’s leading specialist in antique pocket watches and vintage wristwatches.
A family business dedicated to the history of timekeeping, we pride ourselves in our rich and diverse range of timekeepers spanning the history of horology from early 16th Century pre-balance spring watches to those flight qualified by NASA.
